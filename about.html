ILYEOB
ABOUT
Ilyeob is a versatile producer and composer who tells stories through his music. Originally from Seoul, South Korea, Ilyeob developed his production skills by working on world music records and received the 2018 Indiestance award in Korea with his reggae group, Acousoljah. After scoring his first film for CJ Entertainment, Toy Soldiers: Fake Men 2 The Complete, Ilyeob moved to Los Angeles to further pursue film scoring. With expertise from groovy reggae to intricate orchestral music, Ilyeob's versatile approach and ability to produce/mix in-house enable him to tell a compelling story in motion pictures.
