ILYEOB
ABOUT
Ilyeob is a South Korean composer and producer based in Los Angeles. A recipient of the 2018 Indiestance Award for outstanding composition, highlights from Ilyeob’s portfolio include CJ Entertainment's Toy Soldiers: Fake Men 2 The Complete and Dalvoice’s Once Again.

Recently, Ilyeob provided additional music for the short film Sparkwheel, directed by Billy Kent and starring Dave Hill and Adam Pally.

With a foundation in world music production, particularly reggae, Ilyeob has broadened his expertise to encompass a wide range of genres. His versatile skill set, spanning from groovy reggae to intricate orchestral music, enables him to craft compelling musical narratives for motion pictures.
